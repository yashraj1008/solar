Net Metering

Net Metering is a billing mechanism that allows you to send excess solar energy generated by your system back into the electrical grid. This energy is then credited to your account, effectively reducing your monthly power bills. It’s an innovative and cost-effective way to make your solar investment even more beneficial.

When your solar panels produce more power than your home or business consumes, the surplus electricity flows back into the grid. Your energy meter runs backward, earning you credits that offset future electricity use—especially at night or during cloudy days when generation is low.

How It Works

  • Solar panels generate clean energy from sunlight.

  • Unused electricity is sent to the power grid through a bi-directional meter.

  • You earn credits for the excess power you contribute.

  • At night, you draw electricity from the grid using your earned credits.

  • End result: lower bills, cleaner energy, and a sustainable lifestyle.

Advantages of Net Metering

  • Substantial reduction in electricity bills.

  • Promotes use of renewable energy and reduces carbon footprint.

  • Ideal for both residential and commercial solar systems.

  • Seamless integration with your existing electrical setup.

  • Ensures continuous power availability without storage dependency.

SolarTech ensures a smooth Net Metering application and installation process. From paperwork and coordination with DISCOM authorities to on-site meter installation, our experts handle it all to give you a hassle-free solar experience.
