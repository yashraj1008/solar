About Us

Powering a Greener Tomorrow with Solar Technologies

Established in 1999, Solar Technologies has been at the forefront of India’s renewable energy revolution. With decades of expertise and an unwavering commitment to quality, we deliver efficient and reliable solar power solutions for homes, businesses, and industries.

Headquartered in Ichalkaranji with a branch in Kolhapur, we specialize in on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid solar systems designed to maximize energy efficiency and sustainability. From design to installation and maintenance — we ensure excellence at every stage.

  • On-Grid & Off-Grid Solutions

  • Customized Solar Power Systems

  • End-to-End Installation & Support

  • Advanced Battery Storage Options

  • Government Approved Vendor (MNRE)

  • Trusted by 5000+ Happy Customers
